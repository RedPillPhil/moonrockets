Please install MetaMask & Connect to Binance Smart Chain

In order to use LiL Moon Rockets, you need download the MetaMask Wallet extension for your browser and connect it to the Binance Smart Chain network.

Visit MetaMask.io for a downloadlink for your browser and you are ready to go!

Please point your MetaMask network to Binance Smart Chain

In order to use LiL Moon Rockets, you need to point your MetaMask Wallet's Network connection to the Binance Smart Chain:

  1. Open MetaMask and click on "Ethereum Mainnet" (next to the fox logo) to open the Network selection dropdown.
  2. Click on "Custom RPC" to add the Binance Smart Chain network.
  3. Fill out the following:
    • Network Name: Binance Smart Chain
    • New RPC Url: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
    • ChainID: 56
    • Symbol: BNB
    • Block Explorer: https://bscscan.com
  4. Hit 'save' to store the Binance Smart Chain settings.


Your wallet will now be connected to the Binance Smartchain. You can always swap networks using the dropdown in MetaMask popup.
